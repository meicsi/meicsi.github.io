Connecting market with customized technology solutions

MEICSi is a fabless semiconductor company providing design services for custom ASICs/SoCs in the fields of RF mmWave/Mixed-Signal/Digital design.

Solid experience in the full product development cycle ensures low risk and time to market edge for our customers. Excellent technical background brings quality and reliability to our solutions.

Solutions to fulfill the needs of telecommunications industry

MEICSi provides full solutions implementing the full chain from DSP/Modem bitstream to Antenna. Solutions address the needs of modern wireless high-rate data communications.
