COOKIES POLICY

What are cookies?

Cookies are text strings created by a server and stored on the hard disk of the computer or device used to access the internet (smartphone, tablet, etc.) and then resent when the same user accesses the internet on subsequent occasions. Cookies enable information to be collected about the user’s browsing of the website. Cookies can have a varying lifespan and be stored permanently on your computer (persistent cookies), or they can have a limited lifespan and be deleted when you close your browser (session cookies). Furthermore, cookies can be installed by the website that you are visiting (first-party cookies) or by other websites (third-party cookies).

Strictly necessary cookies are cookies essential for the operation of the website and for you to browse the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the site. These cookies are technically necessary for the connection to the website or to provide the internet service requested by the user himself, such as indicatively cookies and related technologies that are necessary for the safety of the user, for the maintenance of the choices of the user regarding the presentation of the website (e.g. select language, save search history etc.). For this type of cookies, no prior explicit consent is required.

How does our website use cookies?

Apart from strictly necessary cookies, our website does not use any other cookies, and therefore, no prior consent is required. In case we make use of first-party and/or third-party technical cookies, session and/or persistent, we shall acquire your prior, written consent.

In case you enter to third-party sites through our website, you should know that these third-party sites are outside our control and therefore governed by the respective cookies policy of each installer.
